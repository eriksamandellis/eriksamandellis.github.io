ERIK, SAM & ELLIS
About
Erik, Sam & Ellis, which comprises guitarist Erik Olson, drummer Sam Russo, and bassist Ellis Green, is a jazz group from Phoenix, Arizona. The trio has performed in various configurations at several venues around the Valley, including Wrigley Mansion, the Phoenician, Sky Harbor airport, and the Nash, the premier jazz club in the Phoenix area. The group performs in both background music and concert settings, and presents music associated with artists including Frank Sinatra and Ella Fitzgerald.

Members of the trio have performed with jazz luminaries including Lewis Nash, Bruce Forman, Michael Kocour, and Jason Palmer.
